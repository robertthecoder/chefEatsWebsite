The Problem / Our Mission

Why did we want to solve this problem?

The problem we see in society is the lack of nutrition in busy individuals and in low income communities. According to a research done by University of Michigan Frankel Cardiovascular Center, low-income communities are more likely to face obesity. Low-income communities are more likely to consume food with low-nutrition or fast food since they are less likely to access to supermarkets or decent restaurants. Busy individuals might also face the same circumstances.

The purpose is to help individuals stay healthy and avoid unhealthy foods that can result in obesity and illnesses. It is similar to other food apps as it services like a combination of Uber (on-demand) and Green Chef (healthy ingredients, but we add functions like calorie tracker and free pickups). In addition, it will be a cheaper alternative to low income and or busy individuals.

How it Works

1. Select Meals from Chefs' Selection:

These are certified, skilled chefs that are near your location, and have a selection ready for you! :)

2. Decide to Pick Up Food or Have it Delivered

Pick Up (free): Make sure chef's address is close to you.

Delivery ($3 -$5): Will coste extra to get delivered.

3. Order the Food

Confirm which food items you would like from the chef and the quantity of each.

4. Order Confirmation

- This confirmation will be stored on your app for reference.

- It shows the pricing, quantity of meals, which meals, pickup time, delivery time

5. Email / SMS Notification

You will receive an email and text of when to pick up or when it will be delivered.
